Privacy Policy for TimerView

 

**Last Updated: February 6, 2026**

 

Introduction

 

This Privacy Policy describes how TimerView ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our time tracking application integrated with QuickBooks Online.

 

Information We Collect

 

Information You Provide

- **Time Entry Data**: Client names, file references, time durations, hourly rates, notes, and billing information that you enter into the App

- **QuickBooks Connection**: Your QuickBooks Online Realm ID (Company ID) and OAuth access tokens

 

Automatically Collected Information

- **Local Application Data**: Preferences, settings, and configuration data stored locally on your Mac

 

How We Use Your Information

 

We use the information collected to:

- Enable time tracking functionality within the App

- Export time entries to your QuickBooks Online account

- Sync customer, employee, and service item data from QuickBooks Online

- Store your preferences and settings locally on your device

 

Data Storage and Security

 

Local Storage

- All time entry data is stored **locally on your Mac** using Apple's SwiftData framework

- Your data never leaves your device except when you explicitly export to QuickBooks Online

- QuickBooks OAuth tokens are stored securely in your Mac's Keychain

 

Third-Party Services

- **QuickBooks Online**: When you export time entries, data is transmitted directly to Intuit's QuickBooks Online API over secure HTTPS connections

- We do not store or transmit your data to any other third-party servers

- We do not maintain any cloud storage or external databases

 

Data Sharing

 

We do NOT:

- Sell your personal information

- Share your data with third parties (except QuickBooks Online as described above)

- Use your data for marketing purposes

- Access your QuickBooks data except as you explicitly direct through the App

 

Your data is shared with QuickBooks Online ONLY when:

- You click "Export to QuickBooks" to upload time entries

- The App fetches customer, employee, or service item lists at your request

- All data transfers are initiated by you and occur over encrypted connections

 

Your Rights and Choices

 

You have the right to:

- **Access Your Data**: All your time tracking data is accessible within the App

- **Delete Your Data**: Use the "Clear All History" feature in Settings to delete all time entries

- **Disconnect QuickBooks**: Disconnect your QuickBooks account at any time through Settings

- **Export Your Data**: Time entries can be exported to QuickBooks at your discretion

 

Data Retention

 

- Time entries are retained locally on your device until you delete them

- QuickBooks credentials are retained in your Keychain until you disconnect

- No data is retained by us on external servers

 

Children's Privacy

 

TimerView is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

 

Changes to This Privacy Policy

 

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

 

QuickBooks Integration

 

This App uses QuickBooks Online APIs to:

- Read customer, employee, and service item information from your QuickBooks company

- Write time activity entries to your QuickBooks company

- All QuickBooks integrations require your explicit authorization via OAuth 2.0

 

We access only the QuickBooks data necessary for time tracking functionality. We do not access financial transactions, bank accounts, or other sensitive QuickBooks data beyond time tracking, customers, and employees.
